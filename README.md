This project provides a comprehensive, visual analysis of the Udemy course catalog, featuring nearly 10,000 courses. The dashboard focuses on key metrics to assess course performance, quality, and market trends.

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):
KPI	                        Significance
Total Courses           :	Measures the scale and overall size of the analyzed course catalog.
Average Rating          :The primary indicator of overall quality and student satisfaction.
Avg. Lectures Per Course:	Reflects the average depth and instructional commitment of the content.
Course Popularity Score :	Quantifies user engagement and a course's market traction.

Key Visualizations:
Chart Type	                                  Significance
Course Creation Trend over time(Line Chart)   	: Identifies the growth and evolution of the catalog over time (e.g., peak production years).
Course Duration vs Avg Ratings (Scatter Chart)  : Examines the correlation between a course's length (hours/lectures) and its perceived quality (rating).
Top 10 Courses by Reviews (Bar Chart)         	: Highlights the most in-demand and commercially successful courses on the platform.
Top 5 Lecturers(Bar Chart)	                    : Assesses the contributions of key instructors based on the number of published lectures.
Course Rating Distribution(Histogram)           : Shows the spread of quality, identifying how many courses fall into top-tier rating buckets.

This dashboard offers a definitive, data-driven perspective on quality, popularity, and trends across the vast Udemy course catalog.By turning raw course data into actionable KPIs and clear visualizations, this project offers a definitive snapshot of performance and potential within the Udemy course landscape.
